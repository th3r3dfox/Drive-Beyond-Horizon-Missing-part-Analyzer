The .log file updates only when a part is attached to a vehicle. If you remove a part, nothing will be logged.

To detect missing parts:

  • Detach and then re-attach a part
  • The log will report only one missing part at a time
  • Until that part is fixed, no other issues will be shown

üîÅ If you want to refresh the log after testing, you must close and reopen the game.

This explanation is based on my personal experience and may not be 100% accurate.
